# Mobility Branch Mastery Guide

The **Mobility branch** is the domain of the fast, the aggressive, and the tactically superior. In ARC Raiders, mobility isn't just about moving fasterâ€”it's about controlling engagements, outmaneuvering opponents, and dictating the pace of combat.

## ðŸ“Š Complete Mobility Node Analysis

### Tier 1: Speed Fundamentals (0-15 points)

**Marathon Runner (5 ranks)**
- Increases sprint speed by 6% per rank
- Total: 30% faster sprinting at max rank
- **Priority**: Max immediately - game-changing

**Sprinter (5 ranks)**
- Reduces stamina cost while sprinting by 10% per rank
- Total: 50% reduced sprint stamina cost at max rank
- **Synergy**: Essential with Marathon Runner

**Adrenaline Rush (5 ranks)**
- Temporarily increase movement speed after taking damage
- Duration: 3 seconds + 0.5 per rank
- Speed boost: 15% + 3% per rank
- **Escape mechanic**: Get out of bad situations

### Tier 2: Combat Mobility (15-36 points)

**Combat Rolls (5 ranks)**
- Reduce dodge roll stamina cost by 15% per rank
- Reduce dodge roll cooldown by 8% per rank
- Increase i-frames (invincibility frames) by 5% per rank
- **Essential**: For combat effectiveness

**Evasive (5 ranks)**
- Increases dodge/dash distance by 10% per rank
- Total: 50% greater dodge distance at max rank
- **Spacial awareness**: Create more separation

**Nimble (5 ranks)**
- Reduces weapon ready delay after sprinting by 20% per rank
- Total: No delay at max rank (instant weapon ready)
- **Combat flow**: Seamless sprint to shooting

### Tier 3: Advanced Maneuvers (36-75 points)

**Free Runner (1 rank, requires 50 Mobility points)**
- Eliminates fall damage from any height
- **Verticality**: Enables aggressive building plays
- **Map control**: Access high-ground advantages

**Ghost (5 ranks)**
- Reduces footstep noise by 20% per rank
- Total: Completely silent at max rank
- **Stealth**: Approach unaware enemies

**Boundless (3 ranks)**
- Vaulting and climbing speed increased by 25% per rank
- Mantle ledge height increased by 15% per rank
- **Map traversal**: Reach places others can't

## ðŸ’¡ Advanced Mobility Techniques

### Perfect Dodge Rolling
**Mechanics**:
- Time rolls with enemy attacks for i-frames
- Practice against predictable enemies (basic ARCs)
- Use audio cues for timing

**Application**:
- Dodge through enemies to flank
- Avoid melee sweeps
- Escape area-of-effect attacks

### Stamina Management
**The 40% Rule**:
- Never let stamina drop below 40%
- Keep reserve for emergency rolls
- Sprint in bursts, not continuously

**Regen Tactics**:
- Use cover to trigger regen
- Crouch walking doesn't cost stamina
- Adrenaline Rush for emergency speed

### Positioning Theory
**High Ground Advantage**:
- Use Free Runner to access rooftops
- Verticality = information advantage
- Force enemies to expose themselves

**Flanking Routes**:
- Plan approaches before engaging
- Use Boundless for unexpected angles
- Ghost enables stealthy positioning

## ðŸ”„ Combining Mobility with Combat

### Sprint-Cancel Shooting
1. Sprint toward target
2. Release sprint key
3. Instant fire (Nimble 5/5)
4. Sprint again
5. Repeat

**Result**: Hard to hit, constant damage

### Roll-Shoot-Roll
1. Combat Roll through attack
2. Fire during recovery animation
3. Roll again to reposition

**Result**: Constantly moving target

### Vertical Hit-and-Run
1. Free Runner to high position
2. Take shots
3. Drop to different level
4. Repeat elsewhere

**Result**: Enemies can't predict your location
