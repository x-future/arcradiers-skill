Wiki & Quick Reference

Branches, mechanics, and quick build guidance.

Branches

  • Conditioning Stamina, shield efficiency, survival sustain. Good for tanks/long fights.
  • Mobility Movement speed, rolls, vaulting, fall mitigation. Best for PvP/flankers.
  • Survival Loot speed, inventory, crafting, stealth. Suited for solo/looters.

Core Mechanics

  • Max points: 75 total (one per level).
  • Prerequisites: unlock parent nodes before children.
  • Category gates: some nodes need category points (e.g., 15 / 36).
  • Ranks: most nodes have 1 or 5 ranks; respect max rank caps.
  • Respec: use the planner to test builds before committing in-game.

Quick Builds

Solo Looter

  • Survival 40–50 pts, light Mobility.
  • Focus: loot speed, backpack capacity, stealth noise reduction.

Team Tank

  • Conditioning 40–50 pts, some Survival.
  • Focus: stamina regen, shield penalties, downed survivability.

PvP Flanker

  • Mobility 45–55 pts, low Conditioning.
  • Focus: sprint/roll stamina, climb/vault speed, fall damage reduction.

How to Use the Builder

  • Pan/Zoom: drag to pan, scroll to zoom (0.4x–2.5x).
  • Allocate: click nodes to add/remove points; watch category totals.
  • Share: use "Share Build" to copy a link with your allocation.
  • AI Assist: ask the Gemini assistant for scenario-based builds.

FAQ

  • Best first picks? Early Mobility (Marathon Runner) then Survival looting.
  • Can I respec? In-game options are limited; plan here first.
