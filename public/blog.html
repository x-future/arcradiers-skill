The Three Skill Branches: Conditioning, Mobility, and Survival

The ARC Raiders Skill Tree is divided into three primary branches, each serving distinct gameplay purposes. This ARC Raiders guide explores each branch in detail to help you build the perfect raider.

CONDITIONING - Stamina & Endurance

The Conditioning branch focuses on stamina management, shield efficiency, and survival capabilities. This branch is essential for players who prefer tank roles, heavy combat, and sustained engagements. Key benefits include:

  • Stamina Regeneration: Skills like "Fight Or Flight" and "Survivor's Stamina" help you recover stamina faster during combat
  • Shield Efficiency: "Used To The Weight" reduces movement penalties from shields
  • Resource Generation: "A Little Extra" generates resources when breaching objects
  • Combat Survival: Skills like "Back On Your Feet" provide health regeneration when critically hurt

MOBILITY - Movement & Agility

The Mobility branch enhances movement speed, climbing, vaulting, and dodge mechanics. Perfect for aggressive players, flankers, and PvP combatants who rely on positioning and speed. Key benefits include:

  • Stamina Efficiency: "Marathon Runner" and "Effortless Roll" reduce stamina costs for movement
  • Enhanced Movement: "Nimble Climber" speeds up climbing and vaulting
  • Advanced Maneuvers: "Vault Spring" allows jumping at the end of vaults
  • Stamina Capacity: "Youthful Lungs" increases maximum stamina

SURVIVAL - Looting & Resources

The Survival branch improves looting speed, inventory capacity, and resource management. Essential for solo raiders, loot specialists, and players who prioritize economic efficiency. Key benefits include:

  • Faster Looting: "Looter's Instincts" and "Looter's Luck" speed up container searches
  • Inventory Expansion: "Broad Shoulders" increases maximum carry capacity
  • Field Crafting: "In-Round Crafting" and "Traveling Tinkerer" enable item crafting during missions
  • Special Abilities: "Security Breach" and "Minesweeper" unlock unique interaction capabilities

Conditioning Skills: Deep Dive

The Conditioning skill tree in the ARC Raiders Skill Tree is your path to becoming a resilient tank. Starting with "Used To The Weight", this branch reduces the movement penalty from shields, making heavy armor more viable. As you progress through the ARC Raiders Skill Tree, you'll unlock powerful stamina regeneration abilities and resource generation skills.

Key Conditioning Skills to Prioritize:

  • Used To The Weight (5 points): Essential starting skill that reduces shield movement penalty
  • Fight Or Flight (5 points): Regains stamina when hurt in combat - crucial for sustained fights
  • Survivor's Stamina (1 point, requires 15 Conditioning points): Faster stamina regen when critically hurt
  • A Little Extra (1 point): Generates resources from breaching - excellent for economy
  • Back On Your Feet (1 point, requires 36 Conditioning points): Health regeneration when critically hurt

The Conditioning tree requires strategic planning due to its category point requirements. Some high-tier skills need 15 or even 36 points invested in Conditioning before they unlock, making this branch ideal for dedicated tank builds.

Mobility Skills: Movement Mastery

Mobility skills in the ARC Raiders Skill Tree transform your raider into a high-speed combatant. The branch begins with "Nimble Climber", which accelerates climbing and vaulting speed. This foundation supports a playstyle focused on rapid repositioning and aggressive engagements. This guide recommends prioritizing Mobility skills for PvP builds.

Essential Mobility Skills:

  • Nimble Climber (5 points): Faster climbing and vaulting - fundamental for vertical gameplay
  • Marathon Runner (5 points): Reduced stamina cost for movement - extends your mobility
  • Youthful Lungs (5 points): Increases max stamina - pairs perfectly with Marathon Runner
  • Carry The Momentum (1 point, requires 15 Mobility points): Free sprinting after Sprint Dodge Roll
  • Vaults on Vaults on Vaults (1 point, requires 36 Mobility points): Vaulting costs no stamina
  • Vault Spring (1 point, requires 36 Mobility points): Jump at the end of vaults for advanced movement

Mobility builds excel in PvP scenarios where positioning and speed determine victory. The combination of reduced stamina costs and increased stamina capacity creates a highly mobile raider capable of outmaneuvering opponents.

Survival Skills: Resource Management

Survival skills in the ARC Raiders Skill Tree optimize your economic efficiency and looting capabilities. Starting with "Agile Croucher", this branch enhances stealth movement and resource acquisition. It's the go-to choice for players who prioritize loot and long-term resource management. This ARC Raiders guide highlights Survival as essential for solo raiders.

Critical Survival Skills:

  • Agile Croucher (5 points): Faster movement while crouching - improves stealth gameplay
  • Looter's Instincts (5 points): Faster container searches - essential for efficient looting
  • In-Round Crafting (1 point): Unlocks field crafting - game-changing for resource management
  • Broad Shoulders (5 points): Increases inventory capacity - more loot per run
  • Security Breach (1 point, requires 36 Survival points): Breach Security Lockers for exclusive loot
  • Minesweeper (1 point, requires 36 Survival points): Defuse mines and explosives safely

The Survival branch offers unique utility skills that can't be found elsewhere. "Security Breach" and "Minesweeper" provide exclusive interaction capabilities, making Survival investments valuable even in hybrid builds.

Best ARC Raiders Skill Tree Builds for Different Playstyles

This guide presents the best ARC Raiders Skill Tree builds for various playstyles. Each build optimizes your 75 skill points differently, focusing on different branches of the ARC Raiders Skill Tree.

Solo Raider Build (Looter Focus)

Distribution: Survival 40-50 points | Mobility 15-20 points | Conditioning 5-10 points

Strategy: Maximize looting efficiency and inventory capacity. Prioritize "Looter's Instincts", "Broad Shoulders", and "In-Round Crafting". Add basic Mobility for escape options. This build excels in solo extraction scenarios where resource gathering is paramount.

Key Skills: Agile Croucher, Looter's Instincts, In-Round Crafting, Broad Shoulders, Security Breach

Team Combat Build (Tank Focus)

Distribution: Conditioning 40-50 points | Survival 20-25 points | Mobility 5-10 points

Strategy: Become the team's damage absorber. Focus on stamina regeneration, shield efficiency, and survival capabilities. "Fight Or Flight" and "Back On Your Feet" keep you in the fight longer. Supplement with Survival for resource generation.

Key Skills: Used To The Weight, Fight Or Flight, Survivor's Stamina, Back On Your Feet, A Little Extra

PvP Combat Build (Flanker Focus)

Distribution: Mobility 45-55 points | Conditioning 10-15 points | Survival 5-10 points

Strategy: Maximize movement speed and agility for aggressive PvP engagements. "Vault Spring" and "Vaults on Vaults on Vaults" create unparalleled mobility. Low Conditioning investment keeps you fast but vulnerable - perfect for hit-and-run tactics.

Key Skills: Nimble Climber, Marathon Runner, Youthful Lungs, Vault Spring, Effortless Roll

Balanced Build (All-Rounder)

Distribution: Mobility 25-30 points | Survival 25-30 points | Conditioning 15-20 points

Strategy: A versatile build that doesn't excel in any area but remains effective across all scenarios. Good for players who want flexibility and haven't committed to a specific playstyle. Prioritize foundational skills from each branch.

Key Skills: Used To The Weight, Nimble Climber, Agile Croucher, Marathon Runner, Looter's Instincts

Early Game Skill Priority (Levels 1-20)

Your first 20 skill points are crucial for establishing your playstyle foundation. Here's the recommended progression:

Universal Early Skills (All Builds):

  1. Nimble Climber (5 points): Essential for navigation and escape
  2. Marathon Runner (5 points): Reduces movement stamina cost - universally useful
  3. Agile Croucher (5 points): Improves stealth and reduces detection risk
  4. Used To The Weight (5 points): If you plan to use shields

Build-Specific Early Priorities:

  • Looter Build: After universal skills, prioritize "Looter's Instincts" and "Revitalizing Squat"
  • Tank Build: Focus on "Fight Or Flight" and "Blast-Born" for combat sustainability
  • PvP Build: Max out "Youthful Lungs" early for increased stamina capacity
Pro Tip: Don't spread your points too thin early. Focus on maxing out 2-3 key skills before branching out. This provides immediate, noticeable benefits rather than marginal improvements across many skills.

Understanding Category Point Requirements

Some high-tier skills in the ARC Raiders Skill Tree require minimum points invested in their category tree. For example:

  • 15-point requirement: Skills like "Survivor's Stamina" and "Carry The Momentum" need 15 points in their respective trees

This guide recommends planning your ARC Raiders Skill Tree build around these requirements. If you want a specific 36-point skill, you need to commit heavily to that branch of the ARC Raiders Skill Tree.
