Frequently Asked Questions

How many skill points do I get?
You receive 75 skill points total - one per level from level 1 to level 75.
What are the three skill branches?
Conditioning (tanking), Mobility (speed), and Survival (looting/crafting).
Can I reset my skill points?
Yes, but respec options are limited in-game. Use our planner to test builds first.
