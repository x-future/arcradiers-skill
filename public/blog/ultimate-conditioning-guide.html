Ultimate Conditioning Branch Guide

The Conditioning branch in ARC Raiders skill tree is the cornerstone of tank-oriented builds focused on sustainability, shield efficiency, and prolonged combat effectiveness. This comprehensive guide will help you master every aspect of Conditioning.

üõ°Ô∏è What is Conditioning?

Conditioning is one of the three main skill branches in ARC Raiders, centered around: - Stamina management and regeneration - Shield efficiency and recovery rates - Damage mitigation and survival sustainability - Downed state resilience and recovery

This branch is ideal for players who prefer: - Frontline combat roles - Supporting teammates with sustained pressure - Long engagements without needing to retreat - Surviving overwhelming odds

üìä Key Conditioning Nodes Breakdown

Tier 1: Foundation (0-15 points)

Iron Constitution (5 ranks) - Increases maximum health by 3% per rank - Total: 15% health increase at max rank - Priority: Essential for all builds

Stamina Reserve (5 ranks) - Increases maximum stamina by 10% per rank - Total: 50% stamina increase at max rank - Best for: Players who frequently sprint and dodge

Quick Recovery (5 ranks) - Reduces stamina regeneration cooldown by 8% per rank - Total: 40% faster stamina recovery at max rank - Critical for: Combat-heavy playstyles

Tier 2: Efficiency (15-36 points)

Shield Efficiency (5 ranks) - Reduces shield delay by 12% per rank - Increases shield recharge rate by 15% per rank - Must-have: For any combat build

Second Wind (3 ranks) - Grants 20% stamina restoration on kill - Cooldown: 30 seconds - Excellent for: Aggressive players

Damage Dampener (5 ranks) - Reduces incoming damage by 3% per rank - Total: 15% damage reduction at max rank - Game-changer: For survivability

Tier 3: Mastery (36-75 points)

Fortress (1 rank, requires 50 Conditioning points) - While above 50% health, gain 25% damage resistance - Condition: Lose effect when health drops below 50% - Power spike: Massive defensive boost

Tenacity (5 ranks) - Reduces downed state duration by 20% per rank - Increases revival speed by 15% per rank - Team utility: Faster recovery means back in action sooner

Last Stand (3 ranks) - When downed, can still move slowly and use secondary weapon - Duration: 10 seconds + 2 per rank - Game-saving: Can still contribute while downed

Early Game (Levels 1-25)

Mid Game (Levels 26-50)

Late Game (Levels 51-75)

Positioning Tips

- Lead the charge - You have the durability to absorb first contact - Hold choke points - Use your damage reduction to control space - Protect supports - Stay close to Survival players for mutual benefit - Time Fortress - Monitor your health to maintain the 50% threshold
