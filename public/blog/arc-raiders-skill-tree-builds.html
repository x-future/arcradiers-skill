The Solo Looter is the highest-efficiency build for solo extraction play. It maximizes Survival investment to unlock top-tier looting nodes while spending just enough on Mobility for basic traversal.

Core Node Priorities:

  • Scrapping Speed I–V — Stack all 5 ranks. This is the backbone of the build.
  • Backpack Mastery I–V — Carry more per run. Higher loot value per extraction.
  • Safe Cracker — Opens locked containers for elite loot without key items.
  • Quiet Hands — Reduced looting audio. ARC units aggro less during extraction.
  • Marathon Runner (Mob) — Base stamina efficiency. Still needed even for non-combat runs.
Best For: Solo queue, economy-focused play, resource farming, avoiding PvP entirely.

Built for team-based squad play. The Tank build allows you to push into dangerous positions, soak fire for teammates, and sustain through prolonged firefights while your squad extracts loot.

Core Node Priorities:

  • Iron Constitution I–V — Stamina regen when taking damage. Essential for tank gameplay.
  • Shield Expert — Eliminates equipped shield speed penalty. Huge QoL at 36+ Conditioning.
  • Thick Skin — Passive damage reduction during Arc waves. Critical for PvE content.
  • Carry Weight I–III (Conditioning) — Support teammates by carrying their overflow loot.
  • Field Medic (Surv) — Faster heal speed lets you recover between engagements.
Best For: 3-player squads, ARC wave defense, team extraction plays, ranked competitive.

How to Choose Your ARC Raiders Build

Ask yourself these three questions:

  1. Do you play solo or in a team? — Solo players need self-sufficient builds. Team players can specialize.
  2. What's your primary goal each raid? — Looting = Survival-heavy. Killing = Mobility-heavy. Sustaining = Conditioning-heavy.
  3. What's your risk tolerance? — High-risk players benefit from PvP Flanker. Conservative players thrive with Solo Looter.
