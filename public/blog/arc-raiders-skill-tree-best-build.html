Why There's a "Best" ARC Raiders Build

With 75 total skill points and 45+ nodes across Conditioning, Mobility, and Survival, most players assume every build is equally valid. In practice, certain nodes provide dramatically higher return-on-investment. The ARC Raiders skill tree best build prioritizes nodes that are universally useful — regardless of whether you're doing solo extraction, team combat, or PvP encounters.

Key insight: Mobility provides the highest floor value for all playstyles. Stamina management directly influences every in-game action — sprinting, vaulting, dodging. This is why the best general build puts roughly 30–35 points into Mobility before specializing.

Priority Skill Nodes — Order of Investment

Tier 1 — Unlock First (Points 1–20)

  • Marathon Runner (Mobility) — Reduces stamina drain while sprinting. Mandatory for all builds.
  • Youthful Lungs (Mobility) — Increases max stamina pool. Amplifies every other stamina node's value.
  • Scrapping Speed I–III (Survival) — Faster looting directly increases your per-raid efficiency.
  • Carry Capacity I (Conditioning) — Extra inventory weight from the start helps every early run.

Tier 2 — Mid-Game Priority (Points 21–50)

  • Effortless Roll (Mobility) — Drastically reduces dodge stamina cost. Transforms PvP survivability.
  • Nimble Climber (Mobility) — Faster vaulting and climbing opens map traversal routes others can't use.
  • Backpack Mastery I–V (Survival) — Each rank adds carry capacity without Conditioning investment.
  • Field Medic (Survival) — Faster heal item usage is a PvE and PvP multiplier.

Tier 3 — End-Game Investments (Points 51–75)

  • Extreme Sprinter (Mobility, requires 36 pts in branch) — Highest DPS-equivalent node in the tree. Sprint speed increase is massive.
  • Safe Cracker (Survival) — Unlocks high-value loot rooms passively.
  • Iron Lungs (Conditioning) — Removes shield equip penalties for late-game loadouts.

Branch-by-Branch Breakdown

Mobility Branch (32 points) — Why So Much?

The Mobility branch in ARC Raiders is the most efficient in terms of points-to-impact ratio. Every stamina-related node has a compounding effect: more stamina → more dodges → more vaults → faster traversal → more consistent escape from bad situations. The ARC Raiders skill tree best build front-loads Mobility because it benefits both PvP (dodging) and PvE (fleeing Arc waves).

Survival Branch (28 points) — The Hidden Power Branch

Most new players undervalue Survival. Faster looting, larger carry capacity, and quicker healing are passive income multipliers. Every raid, Survival nodes silently increase your loot value by 20–35% compared to a Survival-neglected build. At 28 points, you unlock the most impactful nodes without needing to complete the branch.

Conditioning Branch (15 points) — Minimum Viable Investment

Conditioning nodes are individually strong but the early nodes are the most valuable. 15 points gives you access to the key stamina regen and shield penalty reduction nodes without over-committing to a tank-only playstyle. This is the sweet spot for all-rounder builds.

Leveling Path: Levels 1–75

  1. Levels 1–15: All points into Mobility. Marathon Runner → Youthful Lungs → first Scrapping Speed node.
  2. Levels 16–30: Split between Mobility and Survival. Reach Effortless Roll (Mob) while building Scrapping/Carry nodes.
  3. Levels 31–50: Alternate between Mobility (Nimble Climber, Stamina nodes) and Survival (Backpack Mastery, Field Medic).
  4. Levels 51–65: Push Mobility to 36 to unlock Extreme Sprinter gate. Fill remaining Survival nodes.
  5. Levels 66–75: Invest remaining points in Conditioning (Iron Lungs, Carry Capacity II) for late-game polish.
Common Mistake: Don't invest heavily into Conditioning before reaching Mobility's Extreme Sprinter node (requires 36 pts in Mobility). Many players stall here and regret it later.
