Mobility Branch Mastery Guide

The Mobility branch is the domain of the fast, the aggressive, and the tactically superior. In ARC Raiders, mobility isn't just about moving faster‚Äîit's about controlling engagements, outmaneuvering opponents, and dictating the pace of combat.

üèÉ What is Mobility?

Mobility focuses on: - Movement speed and sprint efficiency - Combat rolls and dodge mechanics - Vaulting and climbing capabilities - Fall damage mitigation for vertical plays

This branch is perfect for: - PvP-focused players - Flankers and harassers - Players who value positioning - Solo survivors

üìä Complete Mobility Node Analysis

Tier 1: Speed Fundamentals (0-15 points)

Marathon Runner (5 ranks) - Increases sprint speed by 6% per rank - Total: 30% faster sprinting at max rank - Priority: Max immediately - game-changing

Sprinter (5 ranks) - Reduces stamina cost while sprinting by 10% per rank - Total: 50% reduced sprint stamina cost at max rank - Synergy: Essential with Marathon Runner

Adrenaline Rush (5 ranks) - Temporarily increase movement speed after taking damage - Duration: 3 seconds + 0.5 per rank - Speed boost: 15% + 3% per rank - Escape mechanic: Get out of bad situations

Tier 2: Combat Mobility (15-36 points)

Combat Rolls (5 ranks) - Reduce dodge roll stamina cost by 15% per rank - Reduce dodge roll cooldown by 8% per rank - Increase i-frames (invincibility frames) by 5% per rank - Essential: For combat effectiveness

Evasive (5 ranks) - Increases dodge/dash distance by 10% per rank - Total: 50% greater dodge distance at max rank - Spacial awareness: Create more separation

Nimble (5 ranks) - Reduces weapon ready delay after sprinting by 20% per rank - Total: No delay at max rank (instant weapon ready) - Combat flow: Seamless sprint to shooting

Tier 3: Advanced Maneuvers (36-75 points)

Free Runner (1 rank, requires 50 Mobility points) - Eliminates fall damage from any height - Verticality: Enables aggressive building plays - Map control: Access high-ground advantages

Ghost (5 ranks) - Reduces footstep noise by 20% per rank - Total: Completely silent at max rank - Stealth: Approach unaware enemies

Boundless (3 ranks) - Vaulting and climbing speed increased by 25% per rank - Mantle ledge height increased by 15% per rank - Map traversal: Reach places others can't

üí° Advanced Mobility Techniques

Perfect Dodge Rolling

Mechanics: - Time rolls with enemy attacks for i-frames - Practice against predictable enemies (basic ARCs) - Use audio cues for timing

Application: - Dodge through enemies to flank - Avoid melee sweeps - Escape area-of-effect attacks

Stamina Management

The 40% Rule: - Never let stamina drop below 40% - Keep reserve for emergency rolls - Sprint in bursts, not continuously

Regen Tactics: - Use cover to trigger regen - Crouch walking doesn't cost stamina - Adrenaline Rush for emergency speed

Positioning Theory

High Ground Advantage: - Use Free Runner to access rooftops - Verticality = information advantage - Force enemies to expose themselves

Flanking Routes: - Plan approaches before engaging - Use Boundless for unexpected angles - Ghost enables stealthy positioning

üîÑ Combining Mobility with Combat

Sprint-Cancel Shooting

1. Sprint toward target 2. Release sprint key 3. Instant fire (Nimble 5/5) 4. Sprint again 5. Repeat

Result: Hard to hit, constant damage

Roll-Shoot-Roll

1. Combat Roll through attack 2. Fire during recovery animation 3. Roll again to reposition

Result: Constantly moving target

Vertical Hit-and-Run

1. Free Runner to high position 2. Take shots 3. Drop to different level 4. Repeat elsewhere

Result: Enemies can't predict your location

üéì Training Exercises

Beginner Exercises

1. Sprint Circles: Sprint around obstacles, maintain momentum 2. Roll Timing: Practice dodging melee attacks 3. Stamina Management: Sprint until 40%, regen, repeat

Intermediate Exercises

1. Perfect Dodges: Dodge 10 attacks in a row without getting hit 2. Speed Routes: Time yourself between points, optimize path 3. Combat Flow: Sprint-shoot-roll-shoot sequences

Advanced Exercises

1. Silent Takedowns: Use Ghost to approach and eliminate without alerting 2. Vertical Mastery: Navigate map using only Free Runner and Boundless 3. 1vX Situations: Win fights while outnumbered using pure mobility

‚ùå Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake 1: Overcommitting to Sprint

Problem: Sprinting everywhere = no stamina for combat Solution: Walk in safe areas, sprint for engagements only

Mistake 2: Ignoring Verticality

Problem: Staying on ground level = limited options Solution: Use Free Runner and Boundless for 3D movement

Mistake 3: Predictable Movement

Problem: Same routes every time = easy to predict Solution: Vary approaches, use unexpected angles

Mistake 4: Forgoing Ghost

Problem: Loud footsteps = give away position Solution: Ghost is essential for stealth approaches
