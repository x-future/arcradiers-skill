Observed Gameplay Evidence

Evidence 1 — Movement Patterns

Extreme Sprint Frequency

In observed stream footage, Tfue consistently out-sprints opponents during third-party situations and disengagements. The sprint speed differential visible on stream is consistent with having the Extreme Sprinter node (requires 36 Mobility points) unlocked and fully ranked.

Evidence 2 — Dodge Economy

High-Volume Dodging in Combat

Tfue's combat style in ARC Raiders involves frequent dodge rolls during engagements — more than baseline stamina would allow without significant Mobility investment. The dodge frequency is consistent with having Effortless Roll (reduced dodge stamina cost) active.

Evidence 3 — Vault Speed

Faster-Than-Baseline Climbing

Vault and climb animations during stream footage appear accelerated compared to default character speed. This is consistent with the Nimble Climber node in the Mobility branch, which reduces vault/climb time.

Evidence 4 — Looting Behavior

Limited Survival Investment Signs

Tfue's looting behavior does not show obvious signs of heavy Survival investment — he loots at what appears to be near-default speed, suggesting he did not prioritize Scrapping Speed nodes. This is consistent with a Mobility-dominant build that treats looting as secondary.

Why a Pro Like Tfue Would Choose This Build

Elite competitive players consistently prioritize inputs that scale with skill. The Mobility branch in ARC Raiders is unique because its nodes amplify mechanical decisions: a better player extracts more value from sprint speed, dodge efficiency, and vault speed than a casual player does. Tfue's documented playstyle across multiple games prioritizes movement as a core competency.

By contrast, Survival nodes (looting speed, carry capacity) and Conditioning nodes (tank sustain) provide more passive value — they work regardless of skill level. High-skill players like Tfue tend to under-invest in passive nodes because they can compensate with skill-based play, and instead maximize nodes that amplify their mechanical advantage.
