Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  ARC Raiders Skill Tree Builder

01 / Overview

Introduction

Welcome to ARC Raiders Skill Tree Builder ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit arcraiderskill.com.

This is a fan-made, community tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the developers of ARC Raiders. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

02 / Data We Collect

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate and improve the site:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, and referral source, collected anonymously via Google Analytics.
  • Build data — skill tree builds are encoded in URL hash fragments and stored only in your browser. We do not store builds on our servers.
  • Cookies — we use essential and analytics cookies. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.
  • Contact information — if you email us, we store your email address solely to reply to your inquiry.

We do not collect names, addresses, payment details, or any sensitive personal data.

03 / Analytics

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as device type, browser, country, and pages visited. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings.

04 / Third-Party Content

Embedded Third-Party Content

Some pages embed YouTube videos. When you play an embedded video, YouTube may collect data about your interaction in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode where possible.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties for marketing purposes.

05 / Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Because we collect very little personal information, most requests can be handled by:

  • Clearing your browser cookies and local storage to remove any site-stored data.
  • Using your browser's private/incognito mode to browse without cookies.
  • Contacting us at the email address below for any other requests.
06 / Data Security

Security

All traffic to this site is served over HTTPS. We do not operate databases containing personal user accounts or passwords. Skill builds are stored only in URL parameters on your device.

07 / Changes

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out — we're happy to help.
